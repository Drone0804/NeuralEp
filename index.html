Q & A

What is Neural Dust?

Neural Dust is currently the size of a grain of sand. This BCI application can fit anywhere in the body mainly in nerves. This technolgy basically monitors your brain activity 24/7. It also detects unusual brain activity such as a seizure.

What is RNS System?

An RNS System has a neurostimulator with tiny leads attatched to it. The leads attatch to the seizure focai part of the brain while the neurostimulator is places at the surface of your brain. When the neurostimulator gets a signal of a seizure, it send a breif pulse of electric stimulation throughout the brain normalizing the brain activty. This means there is no seizure.

What is the final Plan?

The final plan is to place the Neural Dust in the seizure focai part of the brain and when it detects any unusual brain activity, it wirelessly send a signal to the neurostimulator and then the neurostimulator sends a brief pulse of electric stimulation normalizing the brain activity. I replaced the leads with Neural Dust because the leads can leave cuts on your brain increasing the chance of internal bleeding in the brain and infection in the brain.
